# About SafeLink Africa

## Founder & Vision

**SafeLink Africa** was founded and created by **Henry Maobughichi Ugochukwu** with a clear vision: to make Africa a safer place through innovative technology solutions.

### The Vision

Henry Maobughichi Ugochukwu envisioned a Pan-African safety platform that would:
- Connect communities across Africa
- Provide instant emergency response capabilities
- Enable community-driven safety reporting
- Protect millions of lives through technology
- Bridge the gap between people and emergency services

### The Mission

To become the leading safety platform in Africa, empowering individuals, families, and communities with tools to protect themselves and their loved ones.

### The Founder

**Henry Maobughichi Ugochukwu** is the owner, founder, and creator of SafeLink Africa. The idea was born from a deep understanding of the safety challenges facing African communities and a commitment to solving them through technology.
